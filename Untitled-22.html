Plume

Plume ranked third among machine-type concealed weapons.When the plume are launched, they are like a peacock spreading its tail, brilliant and brilliant, but when it is dazzled, it has already killed people. The plume is cylindrical, one foot long, and has two buttons on it. The first button can instantly raise the machine gun, and the second button can be used to launch concealed weapons. There are 365 hidden weapons in the plume, with a total of twelve categories. These twelve categories have twelve different functions such as puncture, explosion, and poison. The most powerful perish needles and seven-killing needles can even kill someone instantly.
