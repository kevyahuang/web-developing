What's about this anime

Tang San is the most talented disciple in the Tang Sect. The elders of the sect had high hopes for him and believed that his future was bright; however, Tang San broke the sect's rules in order to obtain the sect's secret knowledge, and this behavior would make him sentenced to death. Tang San was satisfied with the improvement of his knowledge. After leaving behind a peerless hidden weapon that no one had ever built, he jumped from "Ghost Seeing Sorrow" and accidentally traveled to another world: Douluo Continent.
