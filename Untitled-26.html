The Tiandou Empire

The Tiandou Empire, located in the north of the Douluo Continent, originally had ten provinces. Later, with the enfeoffment of the four kingdoms, six forces were formed. The empire itself directly controls five provinces, and each of the four kingdoms controls one. The principality, second only to the kingdom, occupies the smallest province in the east of the empire. The capital of the Tiandou Empire is Tiandou City, located northeast of the center of the Tiandou Empire. It is the core of the political power of the entire Tiandou Empire and one of the two largest cities on the entire Douluo Continent. It also includes small kingdoms such as the Kingdom of Barak and the Province of Fasno.Tiandou Empire and Star luo Empire won the battle of Jialing Pass against Wuhun Palace.

The old king of this empire before the war:

The king of the Tiandou Empire,Xue Ye,died from the poison poisoned by Qian Renxue, the young master of Wuhun Palace who pretended to be Prince Xue Qinghe and the enshrinement of Wuhun Palace.

The original royal highness, Xue Qinghe:

Xue Qinghe,one of the princes of the old king.20 years ago,Qian Renxue, the young master of Wuhun Palace, pretended to be him and tried to make the Tiandou Empire become a part of Wuhun Palace.

The new king of the empire after the war:

The fourth prince,Xue Beng.After Xue Ye had been poisoned, he discovered that the prince was being impersonated and made him the heir to the throne.
